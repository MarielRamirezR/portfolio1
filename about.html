About me

My professional journey has been anything but linear. Over the past 10 years, I’ve discovered that design has the power to create a better world.

Career journey

From marketing and operations to design leadership.

2015

Marketing, leadership, and community

With a background in Marketing and Communications, my career began in 2015 as the Director of Marketing for AIESEC in my town. Leading a team for the first time taught me valuable lessons in customer experience, public relations, and social media strategy.

Hackathons

Solving problems with diverse teams

Around the same time, I started organizing hackathons, collaborating with diverse stakeholders, including government entities and organizing teams, and discovering my passion for solving problems.

Operations

Building processes and systems

This journey allowed me to excel as the COO of Experiencia México, a travel startup where I became an expert in process creation and optimization. I later applied this knowledge in a French startup, combining marketing, design, and motion graphics.

Creative range

Blending creativity with data

Driven by curiosity, I explored various creative roles—graphic design, photography, filmmaking, social media management and motion graphics—before landing in a space where I could blend creativity with data-driven decision-making.

Today

Design leadership grounded in curiosity, collaboration, and human behavior.

Today, I am a design leader passionate about problem-solving, team collaboration, and continuous learning. I have deepened my expertise in interaction design, information architecture, research, spatial design, workshop facilitation, negotiation, project management and rapid prototyping.

I’m committed to challenging the status quo through innovative, impactful solutions and creating a better future through design.

My biggest goal is to keep mentoring other designers, sharing what I’ve learned, never stop learning myself, and take on exciting projects where I can work with different teams and create amazing solutions through design.
